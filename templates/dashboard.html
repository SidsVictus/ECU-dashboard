Connect Your OBD2 Adapter
IC Engine Diagnostic Dashboard — Setup
  • 1 Plug the OBD2 diagnostic cable into your bike's OBD2 port — usually under the seat or near the ECU.
  • 2 Connect the adapter's USB or Bluetooth end to your laptop.
  • 3 Turn the bike's ignition ON. The engine may be off or running.
  • 4 Run the Python server: python dashboard_server.py
  • 5 Wait for the terminal to confirm Engine simulator running, then click below.
No OBD2 device? The dashboard will run with simulated engine data — perfect for testing and practice.
